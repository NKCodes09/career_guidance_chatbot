Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this AI Career Coach unique?

It combines an AI career chat, CV analyser, mock interview quiz, and cover letter generator — all in one place, powered by Gemini AI. It works for any industry, not just tech.

Do I need to pay anything?

No — the platform is completely free to use. Simply create an account and start exploring all the features immediately.

How does the CV Analyser work?

You paste your CV text into the analyser and our AI reviews it — identifying your key strengths, skill gaps, best-fit career matches, and giving you specific next steps to improve your employability.

What is the mock interview quiz?

The interview prep tool generates 10 multiple-choice questions tailored to the job role or industry you specify. After each question you can see the correct answer and an explanation to help you learn.

Can it help with non-tech careers?

Absolutely. The AI is specifically designed to support all career paths — healthcare, business, creative industries, trades, education, finance, and more.
